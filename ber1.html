Guts – The Black Swordsman
Full Name: Guts
Aliases: The Black Swordsman, The Struggler, The Captain of the Band of the Hawk (former)
Weapon: Dragonslayer (massive greatsword)
Companion: Puck (elf), Casca, and others
Symbol: The Brand of Sacrifice

Personality
Guts is defined by his unyielding willpower and determination to keep fighting no matter the odds. He’s stubborn, hot-headed, and often brutally honest. Despite his rage and violent nature, he deeply cares for his companions and especially protects Casca, the woman he loves. He embodies the theme of “struggling against fate.”
Background
Guts was born from the corpse of his mother, and raised by mercenaries in a cruel world. From a young age, he learned how to fight to survive, wielding weapons larger than himself. He eventually joined Griffith’s Band of the Hawk, where he became one of the strongest warriors and Griffith’s right hand. After the Eclipse (a tragic event where Griffith sacrifices the Hawks), Guts is branded with the Brand of Sacrifice, marking him as prey for demons. Since then, he has been on a journey of vengeance against Griffith and the God Hand.
Abilities & Strengths
Superhuman strength & endurance – able to swing the gigantic Dragonslayer effortlessly.
Combat genius – Guts can fight multiple enemies at once, including apostles (demon-like beings).

Berserker Armor – a cursed armor that gives him monstrous strength but damages his body.

Tactical mind – despite his rage, Guts is clever and adapts quickly in battle.

Relationships

Casca – His lover, traumatized after the Eclipse. Protecting her becomes his main goal.

Griffith – Once his closest friend, later his greatest enemy after the betrayal.

Puck – A comic-relief elf who travels with him, balancing Guts’ darkness.

Band of the Hawk (old) – They were like family to him, but most were killed in the Eclipse.

Themes
Guts represents the struggle of humanity against fate, despair, and darkness. His story is about whether a man can keep his humanity in a world filled with monsters. His character arc is one of pain, rage, hope, and love.
